Lasagna

Description

This Lasagna is great for a nice evening dinner. It is for five people.

Ingredients

  • Pasta plates
  • Dried tomatos
  • Cheese
  • Cream
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Put the pasta plates inside a baking tin
  2. Cook the meat
  3. Put the cooked meat on the pasta plates
  4. Put the dried tomatos on the meat
  5. Start again with the pasta plates and finish with ne dried tomatos
  6. Put on some cheese
  7. Leave it in the oven on 200 Degrees for 45 minutes
